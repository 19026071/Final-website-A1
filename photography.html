Safe Teen Driving Campaign

This shoot accompanied a safe teen driving awarness school project in 2018. The visual concept was menacing, thrilling and dangerous to bring an emotive reaction in the viewer. Model, Burton Mackay is shot on a NIKON D3100 in Wellington, NZ.


Consumerism Awareness Photography

This shoot was also a school project in 2018. The concept was the idea of consumerism and the impact it has on our environment. A range of techniques from studio, to street and posed photography was used. Shot on NIKON D3100 in Wellington, NZ.
